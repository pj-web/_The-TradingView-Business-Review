Integrations and Case studies

We welcome our newest partnership with eToro, a globally renowned trading platform. This collaboration gives eToro’s global user base unfettered access to our cutting-edge charting technology, including an arsenal of analytical tools from trend lines to annotations, indicators and precise alerts.

 
 

Here are some other key details coming from our partnership.

 

  • Advanced charting library: a repository of over 100 indicators for conducting intricate market analyses
  • Diverse drawing tools: more than 50 drawing tools for tailoring precise technical analyses
  • Sophisticated analytical capabilities: encompassing extended Fibonacci, Gann, and Elliot tools, facilitating unparalleled market research and strategy formulation

We’ve got news for crypto traders… TradingView Advanced Charts are now a part of the CoinDesk interface! This provides users with real-time quotes and dynamic software for market analysis.

 
 

CoinDesk is one of the most well-known media, events, indices and data companies for the global crypto economy. CoinDesk Indices (CDI), a subsidiary of CoinDesk, has been the leading provider of digital asset indices by AUM since 2014.

 

With the integration of TradingView into CoinDesk, traders gain access to the necessary crypto data displayed on interactive, responsive, and high-performance charts. Supplied with technical indicators and drawing tools, they will help perform more in-depth financial research and prepare for crypto market movements

TradingView now partners with Public to integrate Advanced Charts capabilities in Public’s platform:

 
 

On Public, traders can diversify their portfolio with stocks, Treasury bills, ETFs, cryptocurrencies, and alternatives such as music royalties. It’s a transparent platform that doesn’t participate in payment for order flow, ensuring trades are executed at suitable prices without any conflicts of interest with their customers.

 

Our Advanced Charts are now integrated in Public’s investing platform, providing traders and investors with real-time quotes on high-performance charts with numerous drawing tools and our core technical indicators.
