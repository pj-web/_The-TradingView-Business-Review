What are Lightweight Сharts™?

 

Advanced Charts is what likely comes to mind first. It's our signature and most popular charting product, powering proprietary platforms with intelligent drawing tools and feature-rich technical analysis.

 

Some find them too complex for product needs, so they choose our Lightweight Charts™ instead. These charts are free, minimalistic, yet highly interactive, and easily embedded into your website.
