Championing trading partnerships

Our trading ecosystem, powered by broker integrations, is expanding. We now reach 2M+ traders via 80+ partners globally, underscoring the demand for retail business growth on TradingView.

 

To mark the milestone and continue our dedication to mutual product-led growth, in this edition, we showcase partner stories of our premier B2B product solution — trading integration.

 

 

What is trading integration?

 

Unlike the charting library, trading integration not only gives access to our charts but makes the broker an integral part of TradingView, envisioning marketing opportunities.

 

It also enhances users' trading capabilities by providing access to proprietary platform tools like custom indicators, market screeners, economic data, and many other features.
