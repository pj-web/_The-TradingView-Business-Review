FTX — “Built by traders, for traders"
 

FTX.com offers many international customers access to crypto assets, and is on the cutting edge of intuitive, cost competitive, powerful trading platforms. Undoubtedly, many crypto traders have been waiting for this launch.

Key benefits

  • Tier-based fee structure for crypto markets based on customers’ 30-day trading volume
  • Trading fees ranging from 0.04% to 0.07%
  • Over 130 coins and crypto assets on the platform
 
Interactive Brokers – our first global multi-asset broker integration!
 

For over 40 years, Interactive Brokers has been known as the global leader in international asset trading, global research products, as well as low fees. Today, it has grown to become one of the biggest securities firms in the world with over $10 billion in consolidated equity capital.

Key benefits

  • Highly competitive commissions and fees
  • The lowest margin trading rates within the industry
  • Access to international markets in 33 countries and 25 currencies
  • Wide range of investment products worldwide
 
Tradier Brokerage
 

Tradier Brokerage offers an equities and options trading platform built from the ground up for today’s trader. With this broker you’ll get access to:

  • Multiple asset classes including stocks, simple options, multileg options
  • Advanced backtesting and trading strategies
  • 24/7 customer service team via phone and email

Becoming a supported brokerage shortens the path the user has to open an account & places trades. Promote your brand, engage with existing customers and get new accounts by providing a significant UX improvement. Being in the Web Platform requires certification & has licensing fees associated with it.
