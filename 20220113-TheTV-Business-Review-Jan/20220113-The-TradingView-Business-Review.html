Many are already familiar with the Lightweight Charts, our newest library launched right before the pandemic. Being a self-hosted solution, it has always required client-side data to be added. Tons of developers got in touch asking if they could also access market data with the Library, and we listened.
 
 
Good News Everyone!
 
Announcing the Lightweight Chart Widget
 
 
Now, there is no need to add data manually on your side, go to the widget page and copy the pre-made code.
 
Choose the data you want from our global market data LIST and take advantage of the new technical features our team added to the basic widget:
 
  • Volume histogram
  • Floating tooltip
  • Different chart types ( Area, Bars, Candles )
  • Custom font family ( Trebuchet MS, Arial, Times etc… )
  • Scale Position modification
  • Scale Type ( Linear, Percentage, Logarithmic )
  • Under line area top and bottom colors change
